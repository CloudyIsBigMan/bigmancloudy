About Me

Projects

Remaking Flappy Bird in 10 Minutes


I used GameMaker Studio 2 to to remake Flappy Bird from scratch in just 10 minutes. I didn't prepare in advance, so I did everything from scratch. I did made a ton of mistakes due the time pressure, but overall the challenge was fun and helped by teaching me cope with the time pressure.

Making a Game for #TeamSeas


In December 2021, YouTubers PolyMars and Barji held a gamejam Called #SeaJam in which you needed to make games promoting #TeamSeas, an initiative started by YouTubers MrBeast and Mark robber in which they took out a pound of trash from the ocean for every $1 they were donated. I made this game in 4-5 days because I had exams at my school.
